Hidden Talent

A hidden talent that I have that many do not know is that I am a great Bachata dancer. Bachata is a genre of Hispanic music that originated in the Dominican Republic and it is a romantic dance that needs a partner. I would consider myself a stiff person when it comes to dancing or just any regular activities, but when that specific genre of music plays I am a completely different person. When I used to be a chambelan for a couple of girls' quinceañeras, I was always the main dancer and did solos with the quinceañera whenever they wanted a choreographed Bachata dance for their big day.
